About OB Montessori Center

OB Montessori Center is a private school that focuses on helping students grow academically, socially, and emotionally. The school follows the Montessori method which encourages independence and creativity.

OBMC offers quality education from Casa to Senior High School and aims to develop future leaders with strong values and skills.
